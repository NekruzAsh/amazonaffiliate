Top 3 Best Gaming Laptops of 2020

By Nekruz Ashrapov

MSI GS65 Stealth Thin - 15.6-inch (The best powerful pricey laptop)

The MSI GS65 still packs a punch to this day. It's specifications are well suited for both high fps and also AAA titles that require powerful GPUs for better visuals in games. If you are looking for a gaming laptop that's both powerful and compact and lighter overall, then MSI GS65 is your choice.

CPU: Intel Core i7-8750H | GPU: GTX 1060 - 2080 Max Q | RAM: 16GB - 32GB DDR4-2666 | DISPLAY: 15.6 (1920 x 1080p) 144Hz | STORAGE: 512GB-1TB SSD | BATTERY: 82 Whr | WEIGHT: 4.14 pounds.

Asus TUF A15 (the best mid-ranged cost gaming laptop)

If you want a gaming laptop not just for gaming but for work, then Asus offers us this mid - ranged gaming laptop for both gaming and for doing editings and photoshop. This gaming laptop is both versatile and can offer lots of strenth with the RTX 2060. It can look pricey but it's worth the pay.

CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 4800H | GPU: Nvidia RTX 2060 | RAM: 16GB DDR4-2666 | DISPLAY: 15.6-inch FHD(1920 x 1080p) IPS 144Hz | STORAGE: 512GB | BATTERY: 90Wh | Weight: 5.07 Ibs.

Acer Predator Helios 300 - 15.6-inch (The best budget gaming laptop)

Acers Predator Helios 300 was adored by gamers during it's GTX 10-series era. Although it gave a heavy gamer look with wide bezels and a bigger model in general. It offered a GTX 1060 6GB graphics card that could lock on 60 fps at max settings in most games. You could find it around for $1000 or even less if it's on sale.
